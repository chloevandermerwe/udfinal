Why is Accessible Design Important?

Unfortunately, there are no official laws that require a web developer to use accessible design. So why do it, then?


People of all ages, abilities, genders, and levels of education should have equal access to information and features on websites that we create.


If one really needs convincing beyond that, on a more selfish note, more people will be exposed to your website if it is available for use for all people. More exposure means more income for businesses.
